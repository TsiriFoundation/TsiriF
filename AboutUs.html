About Us

The founder, director and CEO of Tsiri Foundation is Tsiri Marcus who started the foundation in the year 2021 being motivated by the desire to create jobs and empower youth. The foundation began its journey by being registered on CIPC in the year 2021 as Private Company and a Sole Proprietory and later converted to NPO under the Social Development on the 28th October 2023. We try by all means to produce a system that gives back to the community. Its core operations are in Polokwane.

With our help not only will people get the opportunities they were short of, but it will help them abstain from substance abuse.

The foundation locates the talent and exposes it. The most important reason for our target market to choose us is because we make it our first priority to extract talent from individuals. Not even a single member will access the funds until we've met our mission statement.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be one of the best Talent Search Organisation in the South Africa. It is also in our best interest to help the South African economy grow. The organisation will help create jobs for the unemployed people.
