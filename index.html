About Us

The founder, director and CEO of Tsiri Foundation is Tsiri Marcus who started the foundation in the year 2021 being motivated by the desire to create jobs and empower youth. The foundation began its journey by being registered on CIPC in the year 2021 as Private Company and later converted to NPO under the Social Development on the 28th October 2023. We try by all means to produce a system that gives back to the community.
